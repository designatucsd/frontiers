About

Design Frontiers is an event consisting of a design sprint and accompanying lightning talks. The event will increase cross collaboration of students across multiple disciplines, and show UC San Diego students, through a hands on experience, how user centered design can apply to various fields. This quarter we will focus on the field of consumer electronics; specifically on TVs and displays.

Talks

Who We Are, How We Work

Meet our teams in San Diego and Tokyo, and learn how we work globally to design new features for Sony's TVs.

User Centered Design

We'll walk you through our user centered design process (researching, thinking, prototyping, testing, and iterating) as your foundation going into design sprints.

Fill the world with emotion through Sony's TVs

How do our customers engage with TVs and other displays now and in the future? We'll share some of our findings so you can do what we do, always going back to the research as you design.
